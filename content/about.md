# Heya!



I'm 星見紅楓 (Hoshimi557), a pseudonymous writer based somewhere in Canada.

I write about politics, economics, technology, transportation, and whatever else crosses my mind. This is 隨筆 — spontaneous writing. Some posts are polished, some are not.  

---

## Background

I speak Mandarin Chinese, English, and a little bit of Cantonese. You can guess my ethnicity. You will probably get it right.

My educational and cultural background is a mix of East and West. I graduated from a prestigious university (I will not name which) in Canada, in Mathematics and Computer Science. However, I probably read too much Philosophy and Politics to be happy, and not enough to claim expertise on anything. Standing at the intersection of cultures and disciplines feels weird sometimes.

Currently my fields of interest include mainly Machine Learning, Computer Music, and Software Engineering.
